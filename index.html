FAQs

What makes the Mahindra Scorpio Classic stand out in the SUV market?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic stands out in the SUV market due to its timeless legacy with a new imposing look, unmatched presence, powerful performance, premium interiors, and a host of built-in technology like 22.86 cm Touch Screen Infotainment With Bluetooth/USB/Aux and Phone Screen Mirroring, Intellipark, Micro Hybrid Technology, Fully Automatic Temperature Control (FATC), and more, blending iconic style and comfort.

What variants is the Mahindra Scorpio Classic available in?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic is available in two variants, the Classic S and Classic S11, that offers built-in technology that suits the various needs of the customer.

What is the current waiting period for Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

Depending on your location, you can get your hands on a brand-new Scorpio Classic in up to 14 weeks. To know more, get in touch with your nearest Mahindra dealership.

What are the key features available in the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

Key features of the Mahindra Scorpio Classic include 22.86 cm Touch Screen Infotainment With Bluetooth/USB/Aux and Phone Screen Mirroring, Static Bending Technology in Headlamps, Micro Hybrid Technology, Fully Automatic Temperature Control (FATC), and much more.

What type of infotainment system is available in the Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic features a 22.86 cm Touch Screen Infotainment With Bluetooth/USB/Aux and Phone Screen Mirroring.

What safety features are available in Mahindra Scorpio Classic?

The Mahindra Scorpio Classic is equipped with dual airbags, Panic Brake Indicator, Collapsible Steering, Engine Immobiliser, and Anti-Theft Warning, ensuring every ride is a safe, stress-free journey.

Where can I service my Mahindra Scorpio Classic? What are some of the service highlights?

Authorised Mahindra service centres are available nationwide. Use our Service Locator tool to find the nearest centre for routine maintenance and repairs. Service highlights include expert technicians, genuine parts, and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment.

Can I exchange my current vehicle while buying a Mahindra Scorpio Classic? How do I go about it?

Yes, you can exchange your current vehicle while purchasing a Mahindra Scorpio Classic. Visit your nearest Mahindra dealership to get your car evaluated and discuss the exchange offers available.
